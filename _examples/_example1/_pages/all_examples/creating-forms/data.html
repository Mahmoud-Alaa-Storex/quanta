Creating Forms

Creating forms in Quanta is just... fun.  

As you probably already guessed, you can create a full featured form by using just Quanta's Tags.

Example 1:
Let's start with a basic example, where we create a personal data form.

[INPUT|showtag|type=string|name=first-name:personal-data-form]
[INPUT|showtag|type=string|name=last-name:personal-data-form]
[INPUT|showtag|type=submit|value=Save:personal-data-form]
[FORM|showtag:personal-data-form]

Example 1: the output

[INPUT|required|type=string|name=first-name:personal-data-form][INPUT|type=string|name=last-name:personal-data-form] [INPUT|name=age|type=select|value=age|empty=Select your age|range=1-120:personal-data-form] [INPUT|name=allow-sharing|type=checkbox|title=Allow us to share your data in our site?|value=allow:personal-data-form] [INPUT|required=ciao|name=birth-date|type=date|title=Select your birth date:personal-data-form] [INPUT|required|type=email|name=email|label=Insert your e-mail:personal-data-form][INPUT|type=submit|value=Save:personal-data-form] [FORM|method=get|action={ATTRIBUTE|name=name}:personal-data-form]
