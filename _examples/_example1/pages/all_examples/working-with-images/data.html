You might want to link an image sometimes. Nesting tag is the solution.

See the output of [LINK|dodo=dodo|title={IMG|showtag:/working-with-images/dodo.jpg}|showtag:home]

[LINK|dodo=dodo|title={IMG:/working-with-images/dodo.jpg}:home]

Yes, it's an image inside a link, and you can click it!
